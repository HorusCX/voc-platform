About Calo UK

Written by the company

Making Healthy Easy

Calo is on a mission to make healthy easy by providing personalised meal plans and daily doorstep delivery. Guided by three core pillarspersonalisation, convenience, and premium positioning.

Calos app-based platform harnesses advanced nutritional algorithms to deliver personalised meal plans that cater to each customers unique dietary preferences and nutrition goals.

With weekly and monthly packages available, the service offers an extensive, rotating menu for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, ensuring variety and flexibility. Daily doorstep delivery, a customisable meals, and the option to add individual meals are just a few of the features that set Calo apart.

With its unwavering commitment to helping people live healthier lives, Calo is poised to become a staple for busy Londoners seeking nutritious, delicious mealsdelivered right to their door.
